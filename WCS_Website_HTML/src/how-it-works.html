How it works

Well-City provides free 8-week long creative courses, such as photography, textiles and pottery, are delivered by the four project partners - ArtCare at Salisbury District Hospital, The Salisbury Museum, Wessex Archaeology and Wiltshire Creative - and facilitated by staff and local artists experienced in supporting people with mental health needs to take part. Courses are available for both adults (over 20s) and young people (14-19).
