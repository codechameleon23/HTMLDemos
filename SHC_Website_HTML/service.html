Service Users Charter

Using our in-house trainer we ensure all of our staff are trained to a high level.We will support you with the care you require for as long as you wish to stay in your own home.

We recognise your right as a person to:

  • Privacy
  • Dignity
  • Freedom of choice
  • Personal safety
  • Control over what happens in your own home

We will ensure that any work undertaken in your home will be:

  • Agreed by yourself
  • Involve as necessary your other carers
  • Changed at your request
  • Be undertaken in a friendly
  • and efficient manner

We believe you have the right to:

  • An explanation of the service we can offer
  • Help and advice on any other services that may assist you
  • Choose the service you want
  • Have any information you give us held in confidence and not to disclose to anyone else without your permission
  • Know that any information held on our computers about you is done so in accordance with the Data Protection Act 1984, and is made readily available at your request.
  • Be involved in a full assessment of your care needs
  • Receive the highest quality care and professionalism from all our staff
  • Expect that all our carers have been properly vetted and trained before being introduced to our service users.
  • Complain about any aspect of our service in accordance

  • Treat your carer as you would wish to be treated

Quality of Service

The following features will give you the confidence that every time your book with Spire Homecare, you can rest assured that you will be receiving the highest quality of service and integrity from our friendly and helpful staff.

Training

Spire Homecare ensures that all our carers are thoroughly trained. They know that our service users' dignity and independence are important to us.

Supervision

You will meet our manager in person from your initial assessment and from time to time as they call in at your home. We like to regularly monitor our quality of care and this also allows us to ensure that our service users are also happy with our standards of care.

Focus

At Spire Homecare, our sole purpose is to allow you to live as independently as possible in your own

Individual Care

Following your initial enquiry our experienced team manager will call you at home. It is through face-to-face discussion that we can ascertain your needs and provide the level of care that you require.

Confidentiality

All our Service Users' personal information is treated in the strictest of confidence.

Complaints Procedure

We encourage our Service Users' to use our established complaints procedure.
