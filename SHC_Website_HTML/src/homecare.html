The Homecare Service

Spire Homecare aim to provide a service that fully satisfies the needs and expectations of the individual in their own home. We understand the importance of being at home, keeping control of your life and remaining as independent as possible. Our Manager will discuss your needs with you and arrange a person centred plan with your agreement, and where appropriate involve your family and other healthcare professionals. We acknowledge that your health and preference may change.

At Spire Homecare, services are flexible and adaptable to your changing needs. Our manager will make visits to your home,

Social Care including companionship and promoting independence

Personal Care including toileting, dressing and washing

Day to Day Tasks including collecting prescriptions and shopping
