About us

Spire Homecare is a provider of services supporting individuals in their homes. We understand that you value your independence and we aim to provide an individual package of care delivered in a non-discriminatory fashion. We fully respect your right to dignity, individuality, and your right to have preferences and make choices that respect your values with regards to religion, culture and sexuality.

At Spire Homecare we ensure that all our staff are supported and trained to a high standard. We endeavour to match out staff skills and personalities to your needs and preferences. All staff are carefully checked with the Disclosure and Barring Service (DBS). Carers are given full training which is ongoing and relevant to changing legislation.
