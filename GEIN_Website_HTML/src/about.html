About Us

Gujarat Energy (the “Gujarat Energy”) is a company incorporated under Companies Act 1956 and which works to uplift the world environment for a better future. Renewable energy being the future source of energy for the world and solar being one of the most potential renewable energy, we at Gujarat Energy Solar targets to reach across the world to secure the environment and make sustainable energy available to all.

Our products meet IEC 61215, IEC 61730, and IEC 61701 standards and certified. Gujarat Energy Solar is recognized by GEDA/ GUVNL in India and an accredited Channel Partners of SURYA Gujarat Yojana. We are also recognized by most of the State Nodal agencies in India as approved manufacturers and supplier of Solar PV Modules and Solar PV Products. Gujarat Energy offers complete Engineering Procurement and Construction (EPC) services with TURNKEY Solution from concept to commissioning of solar power plant and projects of any size in Gujarat as well as in other parts of India.

National presence of Gujarat Energy with Premium Quality, Timely Delivery, Cost Effectiveness and Customer Satisfaction helped us to become a recognized brand in India as well as in other parts of the world eventually pushing us to grow at a faster pace.
