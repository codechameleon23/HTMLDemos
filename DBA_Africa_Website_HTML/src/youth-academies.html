Athletics academy

Our athletics academy introduces children from 10 to 18 years old to all athletics disciplines. We also offer specialised training and coaching for selected children from within our academy, growing their skills further. We have academies across Kenya with a growing number of partnerships with schools and universities

Rugby academy

We started our first rugby academy in Nairobi and have since expanded with many rugby club partnerships around Kenya. We have partnered with the KRU and hired Andrew Amonde to head up our rugby academies. We aim to grow our academies in Uganda and currently have one in Kampala.

Lacrosse academy

We have established Lacrosse academies in Nairobi and Mumias, coaching children and young adults and are looking to include Silverbacks, a Kampala based club. The sport is growing in Kenya with an increasing number of schools taking up the sport.
