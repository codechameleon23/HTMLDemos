Support an Athlete

Through DBA Africa you have the unique opportunity to sponsor one of our athletes or academy programmes. You can be a part of their incredible journey on the world stage, or watch them develop through our youth programmes. Every penny goes directly to the athlete, that is our promise. Gifting a support package to an athlete would be an ideal birthday or Christmas gift to the special loved ones in your life.

You will receive updates and personal communication from your athlete and watch them in person or on TV in years to come knowing you played an integral part of their success.
