Apprenticeship in Salisbury

This website is aimed, primarily, at young people who are considering their future careers. But it should also be of interest to parents and carers as well as teachers and careers staff. We also hope that it will inspire more local businesses to get involved with apprenticeships.

We're not your usual Apprenticeship website
We're not your usual Apprenticeship website We are different from other Apprenticeship listings that only show current vacancies. Vacancies are listed, but we also look at what's in the pipeline because you want to be able to plan ahead and have plenty of time to consider your options. You need to know what might be on offer in 6 months or a year's time.

And our emphasis is on 'Local'. We want you to know about all the exciting opportunities open to you - here in Salisbury - both for school leavers and for those wanting to return to work in Salisbury after going to university
