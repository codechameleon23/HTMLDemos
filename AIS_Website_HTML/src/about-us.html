SEB Partnership

Salisbury's main business organisations, the Chamber of Commerce, Salisbury BID and Sarum Rotary, have come together with all the local secondary schools and colleges to create the Salisbury Education and Business Partnership.

The Partnership provides the opportunity for young people in the city to interact with local businesses and learn about the opportunities available to them. Businesses can get involved by helping with presentations, mock interviews, career fairs and work experience. It's an opportunity to help young people as they make key decisions about their future, and to present positive business role models. The Partnership always welcomes new businesses and individuals with business experience. To join up complete the form here.
