Busy Bees Nurseries

About

Busy Bees is a fantastic place to work. We offer Early Years apprenticeships, which are a great way to learn while you earn. We welcome applications from everyone, whoever you are and whatever your background.

This mixture of practical, technical and academic skills and knowledge is ideally learnt through the Degree Apprenticeship route.

With over 30 years' experience, Busy Bees provides quality childcare for children aged three months to five years throughout the UK. While we've grown to become the UK's leading Early Years provider, we were founded by three families looking for better, more enriching childcare, and three decades later we still hold the same values at heart. Our childcare apprenticeships provide both an exciting and educational route into childcare.

The apprenticeships we offer are:
Early Years Practitioner Level 2
Early Years Educator Level 3

We start recruiting in January for apprentices that will begin the following September. There are 2 Busy Bees Day Nursery in Salisbury - one on London Road, conveniently located close to the centre of Salisbury along the A30, next to the Parkwood Leisure Centre and the London Road Park and Ride. And Busy Bees York House based in New Street in the centre of Salisbury.

What apprenticeships are you likely to offer in 2022?

• Early Years Practitioner Level 2
• Early Years Educator Level 3
• Both Education and Childcare
