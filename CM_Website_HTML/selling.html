SELLING WITH US

Local experts. Exceptional service.

Carter May Estate Agents

Salisbury’s award winning and number one estate agency.

As you contemplate selling your home, you might think it’s going to be a complicated process. But we’re here to make your move easy – and, above all, to make sure you get the best possible price.

We start with a free valuation, based on extensive knowledge of the local area. Then we put together a custom strategy for a successful sale, designed to suit your property and your personal situation. There are no fees until you exchange contracts on the sale – and if there’s no sale, there’s no fee.

And as well as selling your property for you, we can also help you find your next home. With in-depth knowledge of Salisbury, Amesbury, Ringwood, Fordingbridge, Alderholt and the New Forest – and with a diverse portfolio of properties across the area – we can support your move from sale to purchase.

PERSONALISED APPROACH

Marketing designed for you

No two property sales are the same, so we create your marketing plan to suit your home and the way you want to sell it. Our aim is always to ensure your property gets maximum exposure across a range of media, including local property supplements, specialist magazines, and on our website and others, such as Rightmove. We also send your property details to our comprehensive list of potential buyers, and prepare a full-colour brochure with high-quality photographs and floorplans.
