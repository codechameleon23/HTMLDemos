Our coaches have several years experience of working within school’s, the local community and within elite elements of the football coaching environment to bring together a blend and style of coaching that can support both children and adults of any age and of any ability, from toddlers through to high performing footballers or sports people.

This mix and blend of different styles and philosophies gives Astrella and the coaches a unique and attention to detail syllabus within

our Director of Coaching
Jody Rivers

Our Director of Coaching, Jody Rivers, has been coaching since 2005 and in this time has coached players from grassroots and professional football club academies through to Premier League player’s.

Jody has visited and studied the methods of several world class coaching programmes, from academy level through to the first team
