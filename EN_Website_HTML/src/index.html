LGBTI & COVID-19

Covid-19 has had a big impact on all our lives.

LGBTI people have had to face up to a new reality which has impacted on our work, rest and play. While the lockdown has been hard to adjust to we recognise that for many the easing of restrictions will pose challenges, with simple instructions changing to become more complex, and rules changing frequently

This microsite is to help LGBTI people navigate those changes and signpost where you or your group can get advice or support.
