Selling and Redeveloping
Land and New Homes

Charles Powell provide expert and honest advice to those who own land and property where there may be the possibility of redevelopment. With the frequently changing dynamics of the property market and planning criteria, it is widely acknowledged that many properties can be worth more in redevelopment terms than as single residences, so receiving expert advice to establish this is vital.

We are fortunate enough to have sold the most interesting range of new homes for both national builders and local developers having provided them with detailed pragmatic advice on the marketing and pricing of their developments.

We are also the first port of call for many national developers in the handling of their part exchange business.
