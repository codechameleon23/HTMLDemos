Stone carving courses & bepsoke inscriptions

Providing excellent quality designed and manufactured memorials, stone work and sculpture and teaching these traditional skills.

We have the flexibility and skills to create and install distinctive and individual stonework suitable for both internal and external spaces: fountains, figurative and abstract sculpture, relief carvings, inscriptions and memorials.

All commissions are hand carved, we work with the client to make an outstanding piece, whether it be a portrait, sculpture, garden furniture or a memorial for a loved one.

The only restriction on what can be made is dictated by the material and the imagination.

Bespoke memorials and inscriptions in stone

We have been working with bereaved families to make a fitting memorial for their loved ones. We work, having no catalogue, to create a design representing and celebrating the life of the deceased. Uniquely, people can be involved in both the design work and if desired/practical can be involved in the carving of the memorial itself.

Stone Carving classes and tuition

We offer both term time and weekend classes for all abilities in a warm, supportive environment. Complete beginners will be guided through the whole process, taught good working practice, tool handling and the basic stone carving techniques. The emphasis for those with some experience will be on improving their technique.
