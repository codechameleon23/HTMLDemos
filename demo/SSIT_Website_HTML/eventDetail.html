Wincanton Golf Tournament & Evening Racing

05 MaY 2016

A great chance to enjoy an afternoon of golf at Wincanton, followed by ever-popular evening racing at this historic Jockeyclub racecourse.

Featuring 9-hole Texas Scramble for teams of 4 players, team entry costs £120 including buffet and 2 complimentary tickets to the racing for each player.

Join in with on-course competitions for Nearest the Pin, Longest Drive & Hidden Prize, and with team prizes for 1st, 2nd and 3rd places in the tournament. Prizes include 8 VIP tickets to a Jockey Club Racecourse Music Night of your choice (eg: Bryan Adams, Will Young, Ricky Martin, The Corrs, Tears For Fears or Busted); 4 free rounds of golf at Wincanton; 4 complimentary Premier Enclosure tickets to any Wincanton Raceday (except Boxing Day).

Programme

  • 1.00pm – Arrival – Tea & Coffee
  • 1.30pm – Tee off
  • 3.30pm approx – Finish
  • 4.15pm – Prize Giving
  • 4.30pm – Tipster Talk with racing pundit Luke Harvey
  • 4.45pm – Finger Buffet
  • 5.50pm – First Race
  • 8.20pm – Last Race

For enquiries and booking, please call Katherine Dalgety at Wincanton Racecourse on
01963 435 843 or katherine.dalgety@thejockeyclub.co.uk
