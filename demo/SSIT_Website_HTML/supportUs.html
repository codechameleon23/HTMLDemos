Support Us

SSIT is a growing charity. We receive no government funding and therefore rely totally on the generosity and efforts of our supporters. To continue our work, assisting people living with spinal cord injury in the South and South West of England and providing resources for The Duke of Cornwall Spinal Treatment Centre, we need your help.
