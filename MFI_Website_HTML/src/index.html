Mortgages made simple

Located in Salisbury City Centre we have an unrivalled knowledge of the local area and have worked with clients from all over the country.

We can help with everything from first time buyers to re-mortgages, from moving home to buy to let and from complex income issues to working with the self employed.

Explore our Mortgage Services

Discover our easy,
fee free process

McFarlane & Iles provides whole of market impartial advice on all mortgages. We choose to be paid by the lender, so do not charge a fee for any of our advice.

Our simple process means less work for you. We will complete detailed research on your behalf and help you through the mortgage application process from start to finish, helping you get the mortgage that best suits your needs.

How we work - a simple four step process:

  • 1 Get in touch with us and we will call you back to discuss your circumstances.
  • 2 Based on our discussions we will research the market and present you with our advice.
  • 3 Once a mortgage has been chosen we will begin the application process.
  • 4 We will liaise with the lender from the start of your application process until completion.

Talk to us about your needs

Get in touch to discover more about how we can help you with your mortgage application and discuss any other needs you may have.
