Coronavirus Restrictions | 14th May 2020

Society staff are working remotely, so please contact us if you need to, using mobile phone numbers and email to help us reply to you more quickly. We can help you with repair and maintenance advice, or with anything to do with your tenancy that you may be worried about.

Repairs Guidance | 28th April 2020

Our Repairs Guidance dated 18th March was delivered to all Society homes. If needed, specialist contractor visits may be possible for technical and urgent matters, so please tell us if you need assistance so that we can plan safe working with you to take account of social distancing, isolation or illness.

Gas Safety Check | 25th March 2020

If you receive a message asking you to contact us about your annual Gas Safety Check, please get in touch as soon as you can to discuss this. It remains very important and we will plan a suitable approach, in discussion with you, based on your situation.
