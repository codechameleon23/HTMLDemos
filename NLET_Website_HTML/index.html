Vision

NLET is a progressive charity where everyone has the opportunity to grow, achieve and improve their life chances through social & economic regeneration, education, training and community based activities.

Our Mission is to build on the talents of our learners.

  • NLET strives to be a key player in social & economic growth, regeneration and community development
  • Raising skills and attainment
  • Delivering excellence
  • To inspire people from all ages, communities and backgrounds to achieve their potential
  • Working in partnership with employers to upskill workforce to meet local and other regions key objectives.
