About our learning Community

NLET Adult and Community Learning is a centre for the teaching and learning of adults. We offer a range of courses for learners from both the United Kingdom and other countries.

Our courses support learners from basic to advanced levels, supporting them in living in the UK, finding a job or going to university or other studies. Our qualified and experienced staff are used to supporting learners at all levels, ages and cultural backgrounds. The individual student needs are at the centre of our practice
