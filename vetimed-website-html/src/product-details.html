Description

Description

Adaptil Diffuser is a plug-in device which helps stop or prevent fear and stress related signs in puppies and adult dogs. It mimics the properties of the natural appeasing pheromone from the lactating bitch, the function of which is to reassure the offspring.

Adaptil Diffuser is delivered through an easy to use plug in the diffuser. It lasts for 4 weeks and is active from 50 to 70 sq.m. The diffuser releases a comforting pheromone in the local environment and should be plugged into the room where the dog spends most of itâs time, perfect stressful behaviour at home.

Adaptil is a synthetic copy of the natural dog appeasing pheromone used by dogs to calm and reassure one another. Adaptil can be a helpful tool in: Helping to promote learning during training and socialisation. Helping puppies or adult dogs settle in new homes. Helping dogs to cope with challenging situations such as staying in kennels, noise phobias and travel.

This product contains:

  1. 1 Adaptil Diffuser Device
  2. 1 Adaptil Liquid Unit

Please note: The Adaptil Plug-In Diffuser is only compatible with the UK power supply. For use in other countries you may require an adaptor.
