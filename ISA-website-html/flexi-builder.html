Business for good

At IS Accountancy we are very proud to support a number of initiatives. It is our belief that as a business we can make a difference beyond the spreadsheet. We are partnered with the global business giving movement B1G1 - Buy 1 Give 1 which means that for every good thing that happens within our business, we pay it forward by making regular contributions to the movement for high impact projects. Together we can help make something great happen.

Impact report

See our impact report page for more details and the projects we are supporting by working with you. It is part of our commitment to become a Business for Good. If you would like to do the same in your business, please give us a call and we can explain how it works or visit www.b1g1.com
